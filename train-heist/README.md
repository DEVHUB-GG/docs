Train Heist is a queue-based criminal activity. A crew breaches the rail network, forces a cargo train to stop and raids its freight. Join the queue and wait for a slot, collect the heist vehicle from a depot, hack an antenna to breach the network, crack the access terminal with the generated code, drive to the interception point and plant a device on the tracks. Once the train is forced to stop, raid the carriages, haul the cargo into your vehicle and deliver it to the drop-off for a payout based on what you bring back.

Only **one** heist runs at a time — everyone else waits in line and is auto-started as the slot frees up. The job can be started from the in-world **Rail Network Contact** NPC or, optionally, from the **Crime Traders** app in [devhub\_laptop](https://devhub.gg/product/7054272).
